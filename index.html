Kcals

Kcals is a business messaging application that I cloned from Slack, using a range of technologies such as React, Redux, Javascript, Ruby, Rails, and PostgreSQL. Kcals enables users to create channels and direct messages to communicate and collaborate effectively, and thanks to Rails Action Cable, messages are received in real-time without needing to refresh message feeds.
